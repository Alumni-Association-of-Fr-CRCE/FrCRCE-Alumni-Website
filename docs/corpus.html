We know that there's a special place in your heart for FR. CRCE - a profound pride. You know you are a part of something much bigger than yourself. What better place to honour the place you used to call home than to give something back? The collective impact of your contributions be it your time, talent and treasure can make a critical difference to your Alma mater. Your contribution and involvement are vital to improving the student experience, creating new ideas and path breaking research, and enabling the ever-improving academic stature of Fr. CRCE.

Your generous gifts will enable us to provide support to

Alumni Association

The Alumni Association constantly strives to bridge the gap between the Institute and the Alumni by organising Alumni Meets and various other Alumni Interaction programs, events and meetings. Your contributions will be a great help to us.

Monetary Scholarships for students

A large number of deserving students are still left without the financial support that they deserve. When a student lacks direction or funding, it can make them feel small in the large world of education. By giving students the resources they need, you're giving them more control of their situation, their goals, and their entire future. Each donation you make removes financial barriers for our students who turn their higher education into a lifetime of opportunity.

Technical Teams

The technical teams of Fr. CRCE are doing great in representing our Institute in National and International competitions. They encounter new challenges in collecting the exorbitant funds they require for their project. Your generous giving will help the students in their expenses and thus boost their confidence in excelling in their respective competitions.

You can show your support to any of the following Technical Teams:

Team Abadha

The team conceptualizes, designs, manufactures and scrutinizes a One-Manned All-Terrain vehicle to represent CRCE at the Baja SAE competition.

Team CFR

The team is the official Formula Racing team of CRCE participating in competitions held by SAE.

Team Robocon

The team participates in the Asia-Pacific Robot contest(ABU Robocon), which is an international Robotics competition.

Team Vaayushastra

The team focuses on designing and building state of the art UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles) for participation in the International SAE Aero Design Competition.

Project Cell

The team focuses on designing real life application technology to solve various problems that arise in our society and participate in the eYantra competition.

Team Mavericks

The team focuses on creating Unmanned Aerial Vehicles which have extensive applications in various fields such as search and rescue operations, disaster management, geographic mapping and more.

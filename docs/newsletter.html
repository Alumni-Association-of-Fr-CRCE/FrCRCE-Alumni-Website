Alumni Newsletter

Keeping this in mind, the Alumni Association of Fr CRCE aims at connecting alumni from different batches of the Institute to reconnect with their alma mater and stay informed about the various activities undertaken by the students as well as some of their fellow alumni.

The alumni newsletter 'CRCE Roots' is thus the product of our humble efforts to build and foster a strong bond between alumni, students and the institute. The Institute's alumni are its lasting legacy and strongest voice. Finding new ways to build an engaged membership base is an integral task of this Alumni Association. The association through this newsletter intends to give a platform for the alumni to share their thoughts, accomplishments and experience.

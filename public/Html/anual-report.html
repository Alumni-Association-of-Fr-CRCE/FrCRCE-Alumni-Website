The committee has organized various events such as the Alumni meet, Spotlight series, Webinars Which help to nurture the Alums and the current students of CRCE and the college indeed.

The Alumni Meet

The Alumni began showing up by 6:30 pm and they were received by the registration team. We had rolled out the red carpets to give them a warm welcome.

The Induction Ceremony was hosted by Ms. Mareena Fernandes, President of the Student Alumni Committee. The Alumni Meet commenced with a walk-through video displayed on the screen. In that video, we enlightened our Alumni about the various developments that have taken place in our college all these years and also presented them with a campus tour. The host then invited our Principal, Dr. Srija Unnikrishnan who proceeded in her speech, the department report highlighting the various milestones reached in the past years by the Institute. The Chairperson of the Alumni Association, Dr. Sapna Prabhu in her address expressed her gratitude for the overwhelming response from the alumni. She introduced her concise report of the Alumni Association, which revealed that several alumni have been contributing to their alma mater by instituting awards, joint projects, course sponsorships, and donations, and appealed to other alumni for their valuable contribution too. Before finishing up her discourse, she revealed the front of the 2nd edition of the 'CRCE Roots' Newsletter and felicitated our Alumni Mr. Suresh Balakrishnan (Stulz CHSPL Foundation), Production batch 1995 who awarded financial aid to meritorious and deserving students.

The time showed up for the felicitation of the 'batch of 1996.'' Mr. Kaviraj Easwaran, Operations Head of the Student Alumni Committee hosted the Silver Jubilee Celebration. This year three branches, Production Engineering, Electronics Engineering, and Computer Engineering were felicitated by the respective dignitaries with a memento. It was a very proud moment to hear about their success and achievements, also the feelings of respect towards their home 'FR. CRCE' was reflected around then.

The Distinguished Alumni Awards were hosted by Mr. Kris Corriea, a Member of the Student Alumni Committee. This year, similar to the last, the distinguished alumni awards were broken into different categories, in particular CRCE Young Achiever, CRCE Entrepreneurial Leader, CRCE Social Impact Leader, and CRCE Top Entertainer. The respective dignitaries gave over the honors to the champs.

The later part of the event was entertained by karaoke performances from our Alumni. Mr. Yohan Mhatre, Alumni Head of the Student Alumni Committee concluded the celebration with a vote of thanks. In general, the event at the college campus was a huge success in its prime motive of Connecting and Networking the alumni and the Institute.

2020-2021

The Alumni Association of Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering (FR.CRCE) works as the connecting link between the current students of our institution and our Alumni. Our constant efforts are to enhance the relationship between them. The committee organizes a lot of activities for the students as well as for the alumni. The committee organized various interactive events during the year 2020-2021. The year 2020-2021 was very difficult due to the outbreak of the pandemic but the Student Alumni Committee surely managed to carry out all the events online and with huge success. The success of the events would not be possible without the support of our Core Committee members, our Alumni, and our dear students.

EVENTS AND CONTRIBUTIONS

The Alumni Meet

The Alumni Meet 2021 was held online on 13th February 2021. Even though due to pandemic the meet was online it attained great success as alumni across the globe joined us virtually. The Silver Jubilee of Computers, Electronics, and Production batch of 1995 was celebrated and the batches were felicitated. We were dignified by the presence of our senior principal and professors. Many alumni participated in this event wholeheartedly. There were many performances held by the alumni as well as the current students. And also the winners of the Distinguished Alumni Awards were announced.

Panel Discussion

Panel discussions were the best informative sessions arranged by the committee for the welfare of the current students. The speakers of the discussions were none other than our alumni. Alumni like Mr. Achal Shah, Ms. Slavvy Coelho, Ms. Mariya Ali, Ms. Janhavi Powale, Mr. Parag Doshi, Mr. Girish Batra, Mr. Subhash Bishnoi, and Ms. Sayli Potdar were the speakers for the panel discussion held. The panel discussions made students aware of “How to equip themselves for further studies” and “Explore their start-up dreams”, and also what should be their plan for execution.

The panel discussion was very useful for the students opting for further studies and also for the students who want to build their own startup. The panel discussions also helped students to get a clear vision about what they want for their bright future. Both the panel discussions were held virtually and the committee received an astonishing response from the students.

Webinar

A Webinar was held by the committee on the topic “Goal-based Financial Planning and Investing”. The speaker of the event was our alumni Mr. Girish Ajgaonkar of Electronics batch 1995. The discussion revolved around some important points like strategies of saving and managing debt effectively, some of the typical mistakes that investors make, and also the basic approach that will allow investors to achieve their financial goals. Also, there was a brief discussion on the current state of the financial markets like the stock market, and the right strategies to manage the volatility in the markets.

The Alumni Association of Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering(FR.CRCE) had set its foundation almost 25 years ago with its sole intention being nurturing the relation between the institute and its alumni with its current students. The activities are organized throughout the year by the alumni committee who are working relentlessly to improve the interaction between alums and the current students.

EVENTS AND CONTRIBUTIONS

The Alumni Meet

An annual Alumni Meet is conducted every year on the first Saturday of January which receives an enthuciastic response. This is a time for the Alumni to come back to their Alma Mater and relive the wonderful years that they spent in the Institute. From the past four years, the celebrations for the Silver Jubilee batch have been conducted at the Alumni meet.

It gives us immense pleasure to mention that Batch 1991 of production celebrating their silver jubilee this year has donated around 2 lakhs towards the purchase of Solidworks,3D CAD design software, analysis software, and product data management software.

Alumni Speak-up

The Alumni Committee launched an initiative named “Alumni Speakup” to provide a platform for inviting the Alumni to deliver talks to present students on various technical and Non-technical topics to gain industry insights from the working professionals. This effort was appreciated by the students.

The event had following speakers: Mr. Pavan Savant (Production-Batch 1994) delivered a talk on “Professional and Personal Excellence”, Mr. Aakar Kale and Mr. Rachit Vijan (IT- Batch 2017) had delivered a session on Entrepreneurship among students under Alumni Speak-up initiative.

Alumni Spotlights

The Alumni Association takes immense pride in alumni success and wishes to showcase their stories to the outside world. The alumni have always added glory to the college by their outstanding performance in different fields and thus serve as outstanding role models for prospective students as well as their fellow alumni.

The Alumni Spotlight brings to the limelight some of the 'rising stars' whose accomplishments have given us countless stories worth sharing. It is a series of videos uploaded every month on our Social Media platforms like YouTube, Instagram and LinkedIn. These videos showcase and recognise some of these global leaders, great employers and corporate citizens.

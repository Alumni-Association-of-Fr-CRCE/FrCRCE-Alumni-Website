Mentorship Program

The Mentorship Program provides the right guidance by conducting webinars and helping the students in deciding a successful career path according to their aptitude and talents. They are enabled to settle down in life on their own with a decent earning and status. Along with providing financial aid, the Alumni can also partake in the mentorship program and share their expertise on any topic that they would like. There could be one on one sessions, webinars, panel discussions etc. The Alumni can also sign-up for mentoring the technical teams of our Institute and can share their ideas to improvise the existing plans in order to design a project that would represent our college in National and International competitions.

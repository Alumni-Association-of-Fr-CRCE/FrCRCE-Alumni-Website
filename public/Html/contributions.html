" Contribution by our Alums "

Mr. Suresh Balakrishnan, Production 1995

Mr. Suresh Balakrishnan is the co-founder and joint managing director of STULZ India. Starting from the year 2018 Mr. BalaKrishnan Awards Monetary Scholarship up to 7 Lakhs to many Meritorious and Economically Backward Students of CRCE through Stulz Foundation. Mr. BalaKrishnan has been doing this work every year for the students of CRCE. He relentlessly lends his hand for meritorious students through his foundation. From what we get makes a living, however from what we give makes a life. Mr.BalaKrishnan is our proud alumni who lightens the burden of many students. He also states the sense of help for the student to help us remember how the nation minds about Future Schooling.

Production Batch, 1991

On the occasion of completing their Silver Jubilee, Production Batch of the year 1991 Donated around INR 2 Lakhs Rupees towards the Advancement of the lab which includes the addition of 3D CAD Design software, Analysis Software, Solidworks and Product Data Management software.

Production Batch, 1994

At the Silver Jubilee celebrations of the Production Batch of the year 1994, the batch donated 1.35 Lakh Rupees. The funds were used for procurement of sports equipment and refurbishment of Students GymKhana, Boy's Common room and Girl's common room as contribution towards facility development for students. The GymKhana was inaugurated during the Alumni Meet 2020 by our Director Rev. Father Valerian D'Souza, our Principal Dr. Srija Unnikrishnan, former Principal Dr. Sesha Iyer and the Production, 1994.

Alumni Corpus

The Alumni Committee arranges for yearly donations towards the student project groups participating in various National and International competitions.

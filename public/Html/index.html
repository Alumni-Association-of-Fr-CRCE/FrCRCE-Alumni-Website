Alumni Spotlight

The Alumni Association takes immense pride in alumni success and wishes to showcase their stories to the outside world. The alumni have always added glory to the college by their outstanding performance in different fields and thus serve as outstanding role models for prospective students as well as their fellow alumni.
The Alumni Spotlight brings to the limelight some of the 'rising stars' whose accomplishments have given us countless stories worth sharing. It is a series of videos uploaded every month on our Social Media platforms like YouTube, Instagram and LinkedIn. These videos showcase and recognise some of these global leaders, great employers and corporate citizens.

Alumni Newsletter

Once a CRCEite, always a CRCEite

Keeping this in mind, the Alumni Association of Fr CRCE aims at connecting alumni from different batches of the Institute to reconnect with their alma mater and stay informed about the various activities undertaken by the students as well as some of their fellow alumni. The alumni newsletter 'CRCE Roots' is thus the product of our humble efforts to build and foster a strong bond between alumni, students and the institute. The Institute's alumni are its lasting legacy and strongest voice. Finding new ways to build an engaged membership base is an integral task of this Alumni Association. The association through this newsletter intends to give a platform for the alumni to share their thoughts, accomplishments and experience.
